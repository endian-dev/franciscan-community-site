We meet on the last Sunday of each month at [Saint Gabriel the Archangel Church 2250 East Maule Ave. Las Vegas, Nevada 89118](https://stgabrielbyzantinecatholicchurch.org/). Saint Gabriel is a Byzantine Rite Catholic Church in communion with Rome. Catholics of all rites are welcome to attend our meetings.

We meet from: 12:30pm to 3:30pm. Our Apostolates are **Catholic Charities of Southern Nevada** and **Catholic Worker**.
