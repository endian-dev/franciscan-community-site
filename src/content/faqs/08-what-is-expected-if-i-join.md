---
question: What is expected of me if I join the Order?
order: 80
published: true
---

In addition to attending Mass on Sundays and holy days of obligation and going to regular confession, Secular Franciscans make a deeper commitment to their Catholic faith by aiming to live a Christ-like life, a Gospel life.

Members of the OFS are encouraged to join the Church in her liturgical prayer life by praying Morning and Evening Prayer from the Divine Office. They also have a commitment to a daily examination of conscience and associated penitential acts, attendance at monthly fraternity meetings, and ongoing spiritual formation.
