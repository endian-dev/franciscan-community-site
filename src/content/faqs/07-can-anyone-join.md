---
question: Can anyone join the Secular Franciscan Order?
order: 70
published: true
---

You can be Franciscan at heart, with a calling toward Franciscan spirituality, without joining the Order.

If you wish to take the step to join the Ordo Franciscanus Saecularis, you should be a practicing Catholic and over 18 years of age. You can be married or single, laity or clergy. Even a pope can be a Secular Franciscan, [as many have been](http://franciscanseculars.com/influential-franciscans/).
