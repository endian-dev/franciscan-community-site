---
question: CAN ANYONE JOIN THE SECULAR FRANCISCAN ORDER?
order: 70
published: true
---

A: You can be a Franciscan at heart, with a calling towards Franciscan Spirituality without joining the Order. However if you wish to take the step to join the Ordo Franciscanus Saecularis you should be a practising Catholic and over 18 years of age. You can be married or single, laity or clergy – even a pope can be a Secular Franciscan [\[as many have been\]](http://franciscanseculars.com/influential-franciscans/)
