---
question: What do Secular Franciscans do?
order: 60
published: true
---

Secular Franciscans live their lives as everyone else does. We live, work, play, praise, pray, sing, hurt, cry, suffer, love, and enjoy life with an intentional focus on our Seraphic Father, Saint Francis. He reminds us to keep Christ in our hearts, God in our thoughts, and to know that the Holy Spirit is within us always.

Secular Franciscans are often involved in parish life. They may be Extraordinary Eucharistic Ministers and Readers at Mass, assist on parish councils, be members of evangelical strategy teams, serve as assistant sacristans, care for flowers, help clean the church, and, whenever possible, share Franciscan spirituality with others.
