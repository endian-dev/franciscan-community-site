---
question: Do you live like monks and nuns in monasteries or convents?
order: 90
published: true
---

No. Members of the OFS live at home and usually meet in fraternity in local church buildings.

The OFS is a secular life. It is the authentic way of living founded by Saint Francis and approved by the Holy See in 1221 for people who did not wish to become religious brothers or sisters, friars or nuns, living in a convent, friary, priory, or abbey.
