---
question: WHAT’S THE BEST WAY TO FIND OUT ABOUT JOINING THE SECULAR FRANCISCANS?
order: 110
published: true
---

A: Perhaps a good way to learn about the OFS is firstly travel around this website and discover who St Francis was, to know he’s more than the garden bird-bath saint. It might be helpful to read [the Rule](http://franciscanseculars.com/secular-franciscan-rule/) and other pages about [discernment](http://franciscanseculars.com/category/discernment/). Then if you’d like to know more visit [CIOFS](http://www.ciofs.org/portal/en/) the International Council of the Secular Franciscan Order – the Ordo Franciscanus Saecularis. Alternatiely email : [hello@franciscanseculars.com](mailto:hello@franciscanseculars.com)
