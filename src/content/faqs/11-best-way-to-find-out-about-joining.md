---
question: What is the best way to find out about joining the Secular Franciscans?
order: 110
published: true
---

A good way to learn about the OFS is to explore this site and discover who St. Francis was, and to know he is more than the garden birdbath saint.

It may be helpful to read [the Rule](http://franciscanseculars.com/secular-franciscan-rule/) and other pages about [discernment](http://franciscanseculars.com/category/discernment/). If you would like to know more, visit [CIOFS](http://www.ciofs.org/portal/en/), the International Council of the Secular Franciscan Order - the Ordo Franciscanus Saecularis.
